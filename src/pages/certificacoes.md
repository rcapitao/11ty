# Certificações

## Privacidade

1. IAPP CIPM (Certified Information Privacy Manager)
2. EXIN DPO (Data Protection Officer)
3. EXIN PDPP (Privacy and Data Protection Professional)
4. EXIN PDPF (Privacy and Data Protection Foundation)
5. EXIN PDPE (Privacy and Data Protection Essential based on LGPD)

## Segurança da Informação

1. EXIN ISO (Information Security Officer)
2. EXIN ISMP (Information Security Management Professional based on ISO/IEC 27001)
3. EXIN ISF (Information Security Foundation ISO/IEC 27001)
